We Are EvaLing

Speech recognition systems are in high demand, and have far-reaching and important applications for the future. However, these systems are most effective when the speaker has a common tongue. Therefore, it is less accurate when recognising Caribbean dialects. This begs the question,

"Which platform is best for me?"

We hope with EvaLing we can help consumers and organisations determine the best speech recognition system for their uses and localisation, and additionally to help further the development of such systems.

Speech Recognition Systems used

Made possible by

IBM's Watson

IBM Watson's Speech to Text service provides an API to add speech transcription capabilities to applications. It combines information about language structure with the composition of the audio signal.

More Info...

Microsoft's Azure

Microsoft Cognitive Services are a set of APIs, SDKs and services available to developers to make their applications more intelligent, engaging and discoverable. The cloud-based Speech API, used here, transcribes audio streams into text that can be displayed to the user.
